About Event

Networks permeate most disciplines of sciences, engineering, economics etc. The workshop aims to bring together, people working in diverse disciplines and provides a common platform to discuss various problems and ideas underlying the theory and applications of networks.


Topics to be covered:

  • Network Science
  • Hands On Experience
  • Open Discussions and Research Ideas
